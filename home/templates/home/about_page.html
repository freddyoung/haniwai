Empowering Change in PNG & Oceania

We are dedicated to uplifting communities across the Pacific through sustainable initiatives, gender equality, and environmental preservation.

Who We Are

Haniwai NGO is a dedicated organization working to empower marginalized communities, preserve indigenous culture, and protect the environment across Papua New Guinea and the South Pacific.

Our Mission

We aim to create sustainable solutions that address **climate change, women’s empowerment, social justice, and economic inclusion** across **PNG and Oceania.**

Our Commitment to Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

2025

Quality Education: Ensure accessible education for marginalized communities.

2027

Gender Equality: Expand women-led initiatives and legal support.

2030

Climate Action: Implement community-driven environmental protection programs.

Our Vision

To create a **just, sustainable, and inclusive future** for Papua New Guinea and the Pacific, where communities thrive with dignity and independence.

Core Values

  • Integrity – We act with honesty and accountability.
  • Empowerment – We uplift communities with knowledge and opportunity.
  • Environmental Responsibility – We protect our planet for future generations.
  • Cultural Preservation – We respect and celebrate indigenous heritage.
