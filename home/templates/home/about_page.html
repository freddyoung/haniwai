{# 2. THE STORY #}

Rooted in Ancestral Wisdom

HANIWAI was born from a simple truth: the people closest to the land are best positioned to protect it.

In Papua New Guinea, women are the backbone of the community and the primary custodians of biodiversity. Yet, their voices are often sidelined in decision-making. We exist to change that narrative.

By combining modern sustainable development practices with thousands of years of indigenous knowledge, we are building a future where humanity and nature thrive together.
