{# 2. THE STORY #}

Rooted in Ancestral Wisdom

HANIWAI was born from a simple truth: the people closest to the land are best positioned to protect it.

In Papua New Guinea, women are the backbone of the community and the primary custodians of biodiversity. Yet, their voices are often sidelined. We exist to change that narrative.

{# 3. MISSION & VISION #}

Our Mission

To empower indigenous women and marginalized communities in PNG through sustainable development and cultural preservation.

Our Vision

A future where humanity and nature thrive together, driven by equity and ancestral wisdom.
