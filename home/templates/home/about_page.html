Who We Are

Haniwai NGO is a dedicated organization working to empower marginalized communities, preserve indigenous culture, and protect the environment across Papua New Guinea and the South Pacific.

Our Mission

We aim to create sustainable solutions that address climate change, women’s empowerment, social justice, and economic inclusion across PNG and Oceania.

Our Commitment to Sustainable Development Goals

2025

Quality Education: Ensure accessible education for marginalized communities.

2027

Gender Equality: Expand women-led initiatives and legal support.

2030

Climate Action: Implement community-driven environmental protection programs.

Our Vision & Core Values

Our vision is to empower indigenous women and marginalized communities in Papua New Guinea through sustainable development, cultural preservation, and environmental protection. We believe in equity, dignity, and a future where humanity and nature thrive together.

  • 🌱 Empowerment
  • 🌍 Sustainability
  • 🤝 Inclusiveness
  • 🌸 Dignity & Respect
  • 💪 Cultural & Environmental Protection
