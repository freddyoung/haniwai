Our Mission

Haniwai (Humanity and Nature Indigenous Women's Association) is a grassroots, indigenous women-led nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering vulnerable communities across Papua New Guinea.

Founded by indigenous women who understand the unique challenges facing our communities, we work to preserve our cultural heritage, protect those in need, and transform lives through sustainable development and empowerment programs.
